Standard Hydraulic Hoses

PIX - PERFORMER® EN 853 1SN, SAE 100 R1S-AT, ISO 1436
Application :
Medium to high pressure hydraulic lines. Suitable for petroleum based hydraulic fluids, synthetic esters, biodegradable hydraulic fluids, water-glycol based fluids and air.
Special Features :
  • Qualified for double impulse cycles (300,000 cylces) to relevant standards -SAE 100 R1S AT / EN 853 1SN / ISO 1436-1SN.
  • High flexibility & light Weight.
  • Easy to handle and route.
  • Special MSHA approved flame resistant outer cover can be provided on request.
Working Temperature : -40ºC to +100ºC (Continuous).
Intermittent Temperature : -40ºC to +120ºC (10% of total working hours).
For air application (Temp. max 70ºC) & pressure above 17 bar, cover must be pin pricked.
Approval / Certifications : DNV, GL, USCG, LR, MED, BV
